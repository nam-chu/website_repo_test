Featured Student Researchers

This section will feature some student researchers and their experiences with the research environment. We plan on mimicking this page from the department of economic’s website. The idea is to students the chance to see a variety of student researchers and connect with them.

Nam Chu

B.MATH/B.ECON 3rd Year

Why did you choose to study Economics at Carleton?

I chose Economics because of the important impact it has in our daily lives such as monetary policy decisions, and I desire to understand and analyze the impact of these economic decisions.

What piece of advice would you give to a student that wants to get involved with academic research?

Being part of the Bank of Canada Governor’s Challenge. I got the opportunity to work with a great team and Professor Raúl Razo-Garcia.

What piece of advice would you give to a student that wants to get involved with academic research?

I intend on pursuing a Master’s degree in Economics.

Where can students reach you?

I intend on pursuing a Master’s degree in Economics.
